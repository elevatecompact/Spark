# Vision: Spark — The Global Stage

## Our Purpose

Empower every person on Earth to create, share, and monetize content that matters. Spark is the global stage where creators and communities converge in real time.

## Core Beliefs

**Creators are the center of the universe.** Every feature, algorithm, and policy we build exists to serve the creator. When creators succeed, the platform succeeds.

**Live is the default format.** Real-time interaction — video, chat, reaction — is more authentic and engaging than pre-recorded content alone. We build for liveness first.

**Global means global.** Spark must work equally well in Mumbai, Nairobi, Săo Paulo, and Tokyo — across devices, network conditions, and languages. Latency, affordability, and inclusivity are non-negotiable.

**Open ecosystems win.** Spark integrates with existing platforms, provides open APIs, and publishes under permissive licenses. We compete on quality of service, not lock-in.

**Trust is the only currency.** Content authenticity, creator verification, data privacy, and algorithmic transparency are foundational. We earn trust through action, not marketing.

## Our North Star

By 2030, Spark will be the primary income source for 10 million creators and will serve 2 billion monthly active users across every country. We will achieve this by relentlessly executing our mission and living our values every day.
