# Security Policy

Spark takes the security of our platform seriously. We appreciate the community's efforts in responsibly disclosing vulnerabilities.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

**Do not report security issues in public issues or pull requests.** Instead, email **security@sparkplatform.com** with the following details:

- Type of vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Affected components and versions
- Any proof of concept or exploit code
- Your contact information for follow-up

You can also report via our private HackerOne program at https://hackerone.com/spark.

## Response Timeline

| Window | Expectation |
|--------|------------|
| 24 hours | Acknowledgment of receipt |
| 72 hours | Triage and severity assessment |
| 5 business days | Patch in development or mitigation plan |
| 30 days | Public disclosure after fix is deployed |

## Scope

In-scope: Spark web application, APIs, mobile apps, infrastructure configuration, authentication systems, and content delivery. Out-of-scope: third-party services, social engineering, physical attacks, and self-inflicted denial of service.

## Safe Harbor

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who follow this policy and act in good faith. We will publicly acknowledge your contribution unless you request anonymity.
