# commerce-service — README

## Overview
The Commerce Service powers the digital goods marketplace on the Titan platform. It manages product catalogs, shopping cart and checkout flows, order management, digital fulfillment, and merchant payouts. Creators can sell digital products directly to their audience including merchandise, digital downloads, exclusive content bundles, and virtual items.

## Purpose
Enable creators to monetize through digital product sales beyond subscriptions and gifts. Supports product creation with variants (size, color, tier), inventory management for limited editions, shopping cart with multi-item purchases, secure checkout flow integrated with the payment service, digital fulfillment via download links and streaming access, order history and tracking for buyers, and automated merchant payouts to creators. Products can be featured on creator storefronts and discovered through the platform marketplace.
