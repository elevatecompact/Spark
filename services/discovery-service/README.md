# discovery-service — README

## Overview
The Discovery Service powers content discovery and curation across the Titan platform. It manages personalized feeds, trending content ranking, category-based browsing, editorial curation, and content collection management. It surfaces relevant content to viewers through multiple discovery surfaces.

## Purpose
Help viewers find content they love through intelligent discovery surfaces. Supports a personalized home feed combining algorithmic recommendations with editorial picks, trending rankings based on engagement velocity (recent viewer spike, chat activity, gift rate), category browsing with hierarchical navigation, curated collections (holiday specials, staff picks, themed playlists), and "because you watched" related content. Combines signals from the recommendation service with editorial curation for a balanced discovery experience.
