# competition-service — Troubleshooting
## Bracket not generating: Too many participants, algorithm timeout, bracket type not supported. Reduce participants, try single elimination, check bracket_engine logs.
## Scores not progressing: Auto-progress cron missed, match dispute unresolved, participant not confirmed. Check match status, resolve disputes, manually progress matches.
## Prize distribution failed: Wallet service unavailable, insufficient prize pool funds, invalid payout method. Verify wallet health, check prize_pool_cents balance, confirm winner payout preferences.
