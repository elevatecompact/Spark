# licensing-service — README

## Overview
The Licensing Service manages content licensing, rights management, and royalty tracking on the Titan platform. It handles license agreements between rights holders and content users, usage tracking for royalty calculation, automated royalty payment distribution, and content usage restriction enforcement.

## Purpose
Enable the platform to legally manage third-party content usage through proper licensing. Supports license agreement creation with customizable terms (usage scope, territory, duration, exclusivity), per-stream or per-usage royalty calculation based on agreed rates, automated royalty payment processing through the wallet service, usage reporting for rights holders showing where and how their content was used, and content restriction enforcement (geo-blocking, time-based exclusivity, platform restrictions). Ensures compliance with copyright laws and licensing contracts.
