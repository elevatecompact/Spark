# community-service — README

## Overview
The Community Service manages fan clubs, interest groups, and community-driven content on the Titan platform. It provides forums, discussion boards, group membership management, community events, and moderation tools for community spaces. Each creator can have official fan communities, and users can create interest-based groups around topics and creators.

## Purpose
Foster community engagement by providing structured social spaces around creators and interests. Supports community creation with customizable branding and rules, tiered membership (public, restricted, private), discussion threads with replies and reactions, pinned announcements from community admins, member roles with granular permissions, and community analytics (active members, post volume, growth rate). Integrates with the moderation service for content safety and the notification service for community activity alerts.
