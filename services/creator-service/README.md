# creator-service — README

## Overview
The Creator Service manages the full lifecycle of content creators on the Titan platform. It handles creator onboarding, identity verification, channel setup, subscription tier configuration, payout preferences, and creator-specific analytics dashboards. This service is the bridge between raw content production and the monetization infrastructure.

## Purpose
Enable creators to establish and manage their digital presence. Provides tools for channel configuration with customizable branding, subscriber management with tiered benefits, payout method selection (PayPal, bank transfer, crypto), content scheduling, and real-time performance dashboards. Integrates with subscription, wallet, and analytics services to power the creator economy.
