# advertising-service — README

## Overview
The Advertising Service manages ad serving, campaign management, and advertising revenue optimization on the Titan platform. It supports multiple ad formats (pre-roll, mid-roll, display, sponsored content), programmatic ad placements, audience targeting, and real-time impression tracking with analytics.

## Purpose
Enable advertisers to reach Titan's audience through targeted ad campaigns while providing creators with monetization opportunities through revenue sharing. Supports self-serve campaign creation with budget controls and targeting options (demographic, category, creator, geo), automated ad placement optimization using real-time bidding inventory management, impression and click tracking with fraud detection, and comprehensive campaign analytics including reach, frequency, CTR, and ROI reporting.
