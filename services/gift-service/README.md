# gift-service — README

## Overview
The Gift Service manages the virtual gifting economy on the Titan platform. It handles sending and receiving virtual gifts (emotes, badges, effects), subscription gifts (pay for someone else's subscription), gift card purchases and redemption, tipping with campaign matching, and gift analytics for creators. Gifts are a core monetization and engagement mechanic.

## Purpose
Facilitate virtual gifting as a primary engagement and revenue driver. Supports a catalog of purchasable gift items with animations and effects, one-time monetary gifts to creators, recurring subscriptions gifted to other users, gift card codes for platform credit, and promotional campaigns where creators match gifts during live streams. Integrates with the wallet service for payment and the notification service for gift alerts.
