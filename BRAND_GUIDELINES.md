# Spark Brand Guidelines

## Logo

The Spark logo consists of a stylized ignition spark mark (the "Spark") followed by the wordmark in our proprietary typeface. The logomark alone may be used on social media avatars and app icons.

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Spark Orange | `#FF6B00` | Primary brand color, CTAs, links |
| Spark Black | `#0A0A0A` | Backgrounds, headers |
| Spark White | `#FFFFFF` | Text on dark backgrounds |
| Spark Gray | `#6B7280` | Secondary text, captions |
| Spark Blue | `#2563EB` | Links, informational elements |
| Spark Green | `#10B981` | Success states, verified badges |
| Spark Red | `#EF4444` | Errors, alerts |

## Typography

- **Headings**: Inter Bold, or system sans-serif fallback
- **Body**: Inter Regular, 16px base, 1.5 line-height
- **Monospace**: JetBrains Mono for code and technical content

## Voice & Tone

- **Direct**: Say what you mean without jargon.
- **Empowering**: Focus on what creators can achieve.
- **Inclusive**: Avoid gendered language and cultural assumptions.
- **Global**: Write for translation — short sentences, active voice, standard idioms.

## Logo Usage

- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "S" on all sides.
- Do not rotate, stretch, recolor, or add effects to the logo.
- On light backgrounds, use the full-color logo. On dark backgrounds, use the white version.
- Minimum size: 24px digital, 0.5 inches print.
