# Creator Platform

The Creator Platform provides streamers, video producers, and content creators with the tools they need to produce, publish, and monetize content. It is the central hub for creator workflows on SPARK.

## Creator Studio

The Creator Studio is the primary interface for content management. It provides a dashboard showing real-time analytics including viewer counts, engagement metrics, revenue data, and audience demographics. The studio supports multi-stream management, allowing creators to broadcast to multiple platforms simultaneously through RTMP ingestion.

## Live Streaming

Live streaming infrastructure uses RTMP ingestion with automatic transcoding to adaptive bitrate streams. Creators can configure stream settings including resolution (up to 4K), bitrate, keyframe interval, and encoding preset. Stream health monitoring alerts creators to issues like dropped frames, high latency, or encoding errors. The stream health dashboard shows real-time metrics.

## Content Management

The content management system handles video uploads, scheduling, metadata editing, and content lifecycle. Creators can schedule premieres, set publish dates, manage playlists, and organize content into series. Bulk operations support efficient management of large content libraries. Content analytics provide per-video metrics including views, watch time, engagement rates, and revenue attribution.

## Audience Tools

Creators have access to audience management tools including subscriber lists, follower analytics, and engagement reports. Community management features include comment moderation, chat controls, and viewer statistics. Creator profiles can be customized with branding, links, and promotional content.
