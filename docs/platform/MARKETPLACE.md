# Marketplace

The SPARK marketplace enables the buying and selling of digital goods within the platform ecosystem, creating additional monetization opportunities for creators and a richer experience for viewers.

## Product Types

Digital merchandise includes custom emotes, badges, profile themes, and chat effects that viewers can purchase to customize their platform experience. Exclusive content includes behind-the-scenes footage, tutorials, presets, and digital downloads that creators can sell directly to their audience. Service listings enable creators to offer services such as shoutouts, collaborations, and personalized content. NFT integration allows creators to mint and sell digital collectibles with blockchain verification.

## Storefronts

Each creator has a customizable storefront where their products are displayed. Storefronts can be organized by category with featured products at the top. Product pages include descriptions, previews, pricing, and reviews. The marketplace directory allows browsing all available products with search and filtering.

## Transaction Flow

Buyers browse products and add them to their cart. The checkout process uses platform credits or direct payment. Digital goods are delivered instantly to the buyer's inventory. Physical goods are processed through a fulfillment pipeline. Refunds are handled according to the product type's refund policy.

## Creator Tools

Creators manage their storefront through the Creator Studio. Product creation tools support setting prices, uploading previews, writing descriptions, and configuring inventory. Sales analytics show revenue, top products, and customer data. Payouts for marketplace sales follow the standard payout schedule with a 10% platform commission.

## Inventory System

The inventory system tracks ownership of digital goods. Each user has an inventory of owned items that can be equipped or displayed. Unique items are tracked individually while consumable items track quantity.
