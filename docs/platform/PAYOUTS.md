# Payouts

The payouts system handles the transfer of earned revenue from creator wallets to their external bank accounts or payment providers.

## Payout Schedule

Standard payouts are processed on a monthly cycle. Revenue earned in a calendar month is available for payout on the 15th of the following month. Payouts under  are automatically rolled over to the next cycle to minimize transaction fees. Creators can request manual payouts at any time for a flat fee. Minimum manual payout amount is .

## Payout Methods

Bank transfers (ACH) are supported for US creators with a 2-3 business day settlement time. International wire transfers support creators in over 50 countries with 3-5 business day settlement. PayPal payouts offer instant settlement for a processing fee. Payoneer is available as an alternative payout method for international creators.

## Payout Processing

The payout pipeline runs daily, processing batches of approved payout requests. Each payout is validated against the creator's available balance, payout method, and compliance requirements. Failed payouts are retried with automatic notifications to the creator. After three failed attempts, the payout is held for manual review.

## Tax Compliance

US creators must complete tax withholding forms (W-9 or W-8BEN) before receiving payouts. The platform generates annual tax forms (1099-NEC for US creators). International creators are responsible for their local tax compliance. Withholding taxes are applied as required by applicable tax treaties.

## Payout Reports

Creators can access detailed payout reports showing payment history, pending payouts, tax documents, and annual earnings summaries. Reports are available for download in CSV and PDF formats.
