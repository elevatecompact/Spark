# Virtual Gifts

Virtual gifts are a core engagement and monetization feature on SPARK, allowing viewers to express appreciation and support for creators during live streams and content interactions.

## Gift Catalog

The gift catalog contains a curated selection of virtual items organized by category and price tier. Free gifts are available to all users at no cost and include basic emotes and reactions. Standard gifts range from 1 to 100 platform credits and include animated stickers, badges, and effects. Premium gifts range from 100 to 10,000 credits with elaborate animations, sound effects, and full-screen effects. Limited edition gifts are released for special events, holidays, and creator milestones.

## Gifting Experience

When a viewer sends a gift during a live stream, a notification appears in the chat with the gift animation. Premium gifts trigger on-screen effects visible to all viewers. Gift leaderboards show top gifters for the current stream, encouraging friendly competition. Gift streaks reward consecutive gifting days with bonus items.

## Gift Processing

Gift purchases deduct platform credits from the viewer's wallet and credit the creator's wallet. Gift revenue is split 70% to the creator and 30% to the platform. High-value gifts trigger real-time notifications to the creator and may be highlighted in the stream overlay.

## Gift Analytics

Creators can view detailed gift analytics including gift revenue, top gifters, gift type distribution, and peak gifting times. This data helps creators optimize their streaming schedule and engagement strategies.
