# Viewer Platform

The Viewer Platform delivers the end-user experience for consuming content, interacting with creators, and engaging with the SPARK community.

## Content Consumption

Viewers can watch content through the web player, mobile apps, or Smart TV applications. The video player supports adaptive bitrate streaming with automatic quality selection based on network conditions. Features include picture-in-picture mode, background playback, cast support, and downloadable content for offline viewing. The player remembers playback position across devices.

## Social Interaction

Viewers can interact with content through likes, comments, shares, and saves. Live streams support real-time chat, reactions, and virtual gifting. The interaction system is designed for high throughput with optimistic UI updates and background synchronization. Social features include following creators, creating and sharing playlists, and participating in community discussions.

## Personalization

The viewer experience is personalized through a recommendation engine that considers watch history, interaction patterns, followed creators, and content categories. The home feed surfaces a mix of content from followed creators, trending content, and personalized recommendations. Notification preferences allow viewers to control what updates they receive and through which channels.

## Accessibility

The platform supports closed captions through automatic speech recognition and manual caption uploads. Keyboard navigation, screen reader support, and high-contrast mode ensure accessibility for users with disabilities. Multiple audio tracks are supported for dubbed content. Video playback speed controls range from 0.5x to 2x.
