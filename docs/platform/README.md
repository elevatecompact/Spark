# Platform Documentation

This directory contains documentation for the SPARK platform's major subsystems and business domains. The platform is organized around several interconnected functional areas that together provide a complete live streaming and content creation ecosystem.

## Platform Areas

**Creator Platform** — Tools and services for content creators including streaming, analytics, and audience management.

**Viewer Platform** — The end-user experience including content discovery, watching, and social interaction features.

**Moderation Platform** — Content moderation, community management, and safety systems.

**Creator Economy** — Monetization infrastructure including subscriptions, virtual gifts, digital wallets, and payouts.

**Discovery and Engagement** — Content discovery, search, trending algorithms, ranking, notifications, and recommendation systems.

**Community Features** — Events, competitions, marketplace, and enterprise features.

Each subsystem has its own dedicated document covering architecture, workflows, and implementation details.
