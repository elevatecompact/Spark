# Creator Economy

The Creator Economy encompasses all monetization features that enable creators to earn revenue from their content and audience. SPARK provides multiple revenue streams that creators can combine based on their audience size, content type, and business model.

## Revenue Streams

**Subscriptions.** Viewers can subscribe to creators at various tier levels, providing recurring revenue. Creators set their subscription prices within platform-defined ranges. Subscription revenue is split between the creator and the platform according to the creator's agreement tier.

**Virtual Gifts.** Viewers purchase virtual gifts using platform currency and send them to creators during live streams. Gifts have different values and animations, creating an engaging gifting experience. Gift revenue is converted to platform currency and deposited in the creator's wallet.

**Tips and Donations.** Viewers can send one-time tips to creators, either during live streams or on content pages. Tips are processed through the platform's payment system with instant deposit to the creator's wallet.

**Pay-per-View.** Creators can set content behind a pay-per-view gate, requiring viewers to pay a one-time fee to access specific content. This model is popular for exclusive content, courses, and special events.

**Affiliate Marketing.** Creators can earn commissions by promoting products through affiliate links. The platform tracks conversions and handles commission payments.

## Revenue Share

Standard creator revenue share is 70% for subscription and gift revenue, with 80% for top-tier creators. Pay-per-view content has a 90% creator share. The platform retains the remainder to cover payment processing, infrastructure, and platform operations.
