# AI Ethics and Responsible AI

Spark is committed to developing and deploying AI that is fair, transparent, accountable, and respectful of human rights. This document outlines the ethical framework governing all AI systems on the platform.

## Core Principles

**1. Fairness and Non-Discrimination**
All AI models are regularly audited for biased outcomes across demographic groups. Training datasets are curated for balanced representation. Disparate impact analysis is mandatory before any model enters production. Recommendation systems include diversity and serendipity constraints to prevent filter bubbles and echo chambers.

**2. Transparency and Explainability**
Every AI decision carries an explanation accessible to affected users. Moderation actions include the specific policy violated and the evidence detected. Recommendations include a brief rationale. Creators receive explanations for AI-generated scores or suggestions. Model cards document intended use, limitations, and evaluation results for every production model.

**3. Human Oversight and Accountability**
Automated decisions are subject to human review when stakes are high (content removal, account sanctions). A human-in-the-loop review process allows appeals and corrections. Every AI system has a designated human accountable for its behavior in production.

**4. Privacy and Data Rights**
AI systems operate on the minimum data necessary for their function. Users have the right to know what data is used for AI training. Opt-out mechanisms exist for model training data contribution. All training data is de-identified and aggregated where possible.

**5. Safety and Robustness**
Models are tested against adversarial inputs, edge cases, and distribution shifts before deployment. Continuous monitoring detects drift, degradation, or unexpected behavior. Rollback procedures ensure rapid response to AI system failures.

## Governance Structure

The AI Ethics Board — composed of internal and external members — reviews and approves all AI systems before launch and conducts quarterly audits of production systems.
